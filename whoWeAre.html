Who We Are

The Society of St. Leo the Great is a Private Clerical Association of the Christian Faithful comprised of diocesan priests. We are headquartered in the Archdiocese of Portland in Oregon and were formed under the authority of Archbishop Alexander K. Sample.

Priests of the Society seek to renew parish life in the Church through a faithful living out of the priestly vocation while living in community.
