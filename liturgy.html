Liturgy

We celebrate the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass and the other sacraments in both the Ordinary and Extraordinary Forms with reverence and according to the rubrics of the Church. By offering opportunities for all licit manner of worship, we welcome all those who seek to encounter the Sacred. The Holy Mass is the core of the life of the parish and the life of priests of the Society.
