Community

Priests of the Society live in community while serving multiple parishes. The celebration of a common Holy Hour with Morning Prayer begins the day and sets the tone for the day’s parish ministry and prayer. A weekly “convivium” is shared by Society priests, beginning with dinner and fellowship and ending with Night Prayer. We seek to grow closer to Christ and to live the Gospel more faithfully in our individual lives and ministry.

Liturgy

We celebrate the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass and the other sacraments in both the Ordinary and Extraordinary Forms with reverence and according to the rubrics of the Church. By offering opportunities for all licit manner of worship, we welcome all those who seek to encounter the Sacred. The Holy Mass is the core of the life of the parish and the life of priests of the Society.

Evangelization

Priests of the Society actively engage in evangelization, reaching out to Catholics who have fallen out of regular practice of the Faith as well as to the community at large. Talks, Bible studies and other events regularly take place in our parishes and serve as opportunities for people to hear God’s plan of salvation proclaimed and to grow in their knowledge and understanding of Christ.

Catechesis

We promote and oversee faithful catechetical programs to ensure that parishioners are taught the full richness of our Faith. A strong knowledge of the Faith serves as a seedbed for Grace, a deeper life of prayer, and the development of the Christian life.
